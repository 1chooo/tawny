I created **PowLaw** in 2025 as a gentle nudge toward compound growth. The idea is simple: most meaningful change does not arrive in bursts — it accumulates from small, repeatable actions.

## The power law mindset

Power-law distributions show up everywhere — wealth, attention, skill. In personal growth, the same pattern holds: a handful of consistent habits tend to drive most of your long-term progress.

PowLaw helps you:

- **Track** daily micro-actions that matter to you
- **Visualize** streaks and momentum over weeks and months
- **Reflect** on what is compounding — and what is not

The product is intentionally minimal. No gamification noise, no infinite feeds — just a quiet place to show up again tomorrow.
