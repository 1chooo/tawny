**Art** is the journal you are reading now — a quiet place for longer thoughts, built as a deliberate contrast between two visual moods.

## Two moods

The **home page** is a brutalist bento grid: black gaps, serif headlines, carousels for tags, upcoming posts, and featured projects.

The **reading experience** is editorial: warm paper tones, generous typography, and MDX shortcodes for interactive guides.

## Stack

- Next.js 16 App Router with `next-intl` for English and Traditional Chinese
- MDX content in `content/posts/` and `content/projects/`
- Tailwind CSS v4 with separate design tokens for bento vs. editorial surfaces
