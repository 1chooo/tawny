Making is a sequence of **small decisions** that refuse to explain themselves until the end.

## Rhythm

Good writing on the web respects line length, not because a rule says so, but because eyes tire when lines stretch forever. You feel it before you measure it.

## Constraints

| Constraint | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Narrow measure | Easier reading |
| Few type styles | Clear hierarchy |
| Generous margins | Calm |

These are design choices you can feel without naming them.
